How to play

↑↓←→
Walk around the plate
Mouse
Look around
Space
Place the shape where you're looking
1–6
Pick a shape from the hotbar
7
Toggle chain mode (opens chain end-piece bar: 1=fist, 2=claw)
+ / -
(In chain mode) more or fewer links · default 5
8
Toggle ball joint (post with sphere — fits in socket)
9
Toggle socket (post with cup — ball snaps in)
H
Toggle hand mode (4-finger glove for figures)
Q / E
Cycle filament color
0 0
Press 0 twice fast = toggle fly mode
R / F
(Fly) Go up / down
P P
Aim at a shape, double-tap P to move it · arrows/R/F · P to drop
X
Delete the shape you're looking at
Backspace
Toggle vertical support (places thin upright rod)
Shift+⌫
Toggle horizontal support (rod points forward)
🎮 Xbox
Sticks: walk/look · RT place · LT delete · A fly · D-pad↑ move shape · RB/LB rise/drop · X color · Y shape · B 2D · Start menu
Ctrl
Toggle 2D shape mode (only circles)
Enter
Toggle sideways: 3D shapes lie on their side
Double-click
Free your mouse to click buttons (and back)
Esc
Pause and free your mouse
